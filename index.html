About Greg

Greg Johnson is a visionary leader, brand strategist, and community builder with more than 25 years of experience shaping influential global brands. His work is driven by a simple belief: people and organizations thrive when clarity replaces complexity.

He is the Founder and Managing Director of Orbital Socket, a brand strategy and design agency, and previously served as President of BooneOakley. Earlier in his career, Greg was a founding team member of the Jordan Brand at Nike and held leadership roles at J. Walter Thompson and Saatchi & Saatchi.

Beyond business, Greg is the creator of Baste, a food brand built on connection and shared experience, and a dedicated civic leader serving on numerous boards focused on education, equity, and economic mobility.

The Book

The Johnson Adventures is a meditation on presence. On what it means to pay attention as life moves children growing, friendships shifting, and love stretching across distance.

Illustrated by Greg’s daughter, Carolina Joy Johnson, the book captures a family journey through the eyes of someone who was too young to remember yet somehow magically remembered everything. What a gift she has been.
