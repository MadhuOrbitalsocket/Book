About Greg

Greg Johnson is a visionary leader, brand strategist, and community builder with more than 25 years of experience shaping influential global brands.

He is the Founder and Managing Director of Orbital Socket and previously served as President of BooneOakley. Earlier in his career, Greg was a founding team member of the Jordan Brand at Nike.

The Book
