About Greg

I’m a thoughtful observer by nature—someone who believes the most meaningful stories often emerge from everyday life. My professional journey has allowed me to work closely with people, ideas, and systems, shaping my appreciation for curiosity, resilience, and connection.

Writing has always been my way of slowing down and making sense of the world. With a balance of wit and reflection, I aim to share stories that feel personal yet familiar. The Johnson Adventures is the culmination of years of learning, listening, and living.

The Book

The Johnson Adventures is a thoughtful, witty journey through curiosity, connection, and the small moments that quietly shape who we become.

These stories explore family, discovery, and resilience—inviting readers to pause, smile, and reflect on their own paths.
